I'm Daniel Kamanga — founder of TaffyDevs. I combine a background in Management and Information Technology with a genuine passion for building things that work. Not just things that look good — things that actually solve problems and deliver results for the businesses I serve.

I moved from Zimbabwe to Poland in 2021, and that cross-cultural experience has shaped everything about how I approach my work. I understand what it means to build trust in a new market, to communicate across cultural boundaries, and to create digital presence that works regardless of where your clients are.

When you work with TaffyDevs, you're not dealing with a faceless agency. You work directly with me — from the first conversation to the final handover. No middlemen, no telephone games, no inflated agency fees.
